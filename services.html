Conference Rooms

Our Ventura conference rooms can accommodate up to 10 individuals for your deposition or other meeting and are equipped with remote connection equipment. Conference rooms are always compilmentary for a deposition and may be rented for other needs.

Trial Reporting

Just Write Court Reporters will provide experienced reporters to handle law & motion, hearing and trials in Ventura County courts. We provide all services including dailies, roughs, and expedites.

Video Services

Our professional legal videographers will record your deposition in-person or remotely. The video can then be synchronized to the certified transcript for use in trial or arbitration.

Transcription

We can take care of all your transcription needs. Our transcribers are experienced and accurate. Please contact us to discuss your individual transcription needs.
